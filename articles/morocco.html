Culture

morocco has a rich culture with a mix of arab, berber, n french influences. the food is really fire, with dishes like couscous n tagine. they also have a lot of festivals n celebrations, like ramadan n eid al-fitr. the music is also epic, with styles like chaabi n gnawa.

Tourism

morocco is a popular tourist destination, with places like marrakech, fez, n chefchaouen. the sahara desert is also a big draw for tourists who wanna ride camels n take selfies with sand dunes. the beaches on the atlantic coast are also epic, especially in agadir.

Economy

morocco's economy is mainly based on agriculture, mining, n tourism. they grow a lot of fruits n veggies, like oranges n olives. they also have a lot of minerals, like phosphates n lead. tourism is a big part of the economy too, with millions of tourists coming every year to check out the sights.
